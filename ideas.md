# Smart Flashcards — Design Brainstorm

## Selected Design: Scholarly Minimal

**Design Movement**: Modern editorial meets academic clarity  
**Core Principles**: Purposeful whitespace, typographic hierarchy, tactile card metaphor, calm focus  
**Color Philosophy**: Teal-blue primary (#2D7DD2), sage green accent (#3BB273), warm white backgrounds — calm, focused, trustworthy  
**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric hero with offset card grid; content sections alternate left/right rhythm  
**Signature Elements**: Card flip shadows, soft gradient washes, thin rule dividers  
**Interaction Philosophy**: Every action feels deliberate — smooth flip animations, progress fills, hover lifts  
**Animation**: Framer Motion entrance fades, 3D card flip on Y-axis, progress bar fill on answer  
**Typography**: DM Serif Display (headings) + Inter (body)
